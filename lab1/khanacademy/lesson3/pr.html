ried chicken

  • Time: 3 h 15 m
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken wings 300 gr
Eggs 2
Cornflakes 100 gr
Breadcrumbs 100 gr
Wheat flour 3 tablespoons
Vegetable oil 300 ml
Paprika -
Garlic powder -
Salt -
Chicken fillet 400 gr

Step 1:How to make chicken like in kfc? Prepare all necessary products. For a crispy breading, we need corn flakes, and they should not be sweet - they are sold in the health food department. Parts of the chicken can be anything: breast fillet, drumstick, most of the wing, and just small pieces of chicken - everything is suitable.

Step 2: Wash the chicken pieces and pat dry.

Step 3:Marinate the chicken in a separate bowl. Add your favorite spices and seasonings to it and leave for 30 minutes. I marinated with paprika, garlic powder and salt.

Step 4:Grind corn flakes, but not into flour, but leaving larger pieces. It is best to use a pusher or rolling pin.

Step 5:Add the breading mixture to the cereal.

Step 6:In a separate bowl, beat the egg with a fork.

Step 7:Dip each piece of chicken first in flour.

Step 8:Then dip well in the egg.

Step 9:And breaded in the breadcrumbs mixture.

Step 10:In a frying pan, heat the oil well, put the breaded chicken pieces and fry over low heat for 10 minutes on each side. The chicken must be fried inside.

Step 11:Lay the cooked chicken pieces on a paper towel to remove excess oil.

Step 12:Our crispy chicken is ready. Enjoy your meal!

Source: 1000.menu

Beef and vegetables

  • Time: 8 h
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Bone in Pork Loin Chops 4 chops (7 oz each)
Onion 1 onion (large)
Sweet Red Pepper 1 large pepper, chopped
Green Pepper 1 large pepper, chopped
Barbecue Sauce 1 cup

Step 1: Place chops in a 4-qt. slow cooker coated with cooking spray. Top with onion, peppers and barbecue sauce. Cover and cook on low 8-10 hours or until pork is tender. If desired, top with chopped fresh parsley.

Shawarma

  • Time: 8 h
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Beef Flank Steak 1 steak (2 lbs)
Green Pepper 1 medium pepper, cut into strips
Sweet Red Pepper 1 medium pepper, cut into strips
Onion 1 medium onion, halved and sliced
Fajita Seasoning Mix 1 envelope
Beer 1/2 cup
Flour Tortillas 8 tortillas (warmed)
Pico de Gallo 1 cup

Step 1: Place first five ingredients in a 5-qt. slow cooker. Pour beer over top. Cook, covered, on low until meat is tender, 6-8 hours.

Step 2: Remove beef from slow cooker. Strain vegetable mixture; return vegetables to slow cooker. (Discard cooking juices or save for another use.) Shred beef with two forks; add to vegetables and heat through. Serve in tortillas with pico de gallo and cilantro.
